How to Use Pronouns

I use neopronouns, fae/faer. Now neopronouns can be difficult to use, so here's how to use them:
Fae in place of say, She or He: "Fae was on the street"
Faer in place of Her or His: "That's faer backpack"
Faer in place of Hers or His: "The book is faer"
Faeself in place of Herself or Himself: "Clove needs to stop blaming faeself"
